Hi, hello, wie geht's?

Lauren Does is run by Designer, Illustrator, occasional Coder and knowledge sharer Lauren Kelly.
As well as designing effective and results driven collateral, my design decisions are guided by my BSc in Psychology. Over the last few years I has enjoyed working on a wide range of projects; from whitepapers for Microsoft, to an infographic booklet for the British Government and illustrating a new psychology test for MIT and Columbia University.

Originally from the UK, I am enjoying living in, and working from Berlin. I've worked both nationally and internationally with clients across the UK, Europe, America and China.

When I'm not designing, I love to share my passion for both design and psychology at Form for Thought. Giving insights on how to design with psychology in mind and writing up the latest in web, graphic and visual design-come-psychology research.
Put the kettle on, pour a cuppa and have a read.

If you are looking for a designer, or a collaboration send me an email at hi@laurendo.es.
