Branding Client: Stylindex

Whitepapers and supporting templates

Holly is a fashion design and stylist. She spotted a gap in the market, providing an app for the fashion industry to easily find, hire and pay industry professionals. Acting as a 'black book' for the industry.


The brief

Developing the idea of creating a black book for the fashion industry we developed an aesthetic that used a framing device, to be used over photography and over the square pattern. Holly needed a full brand package, from logo to typefaces, colour pallete and wider brand design. As well as focusing on the collateral around the brand, we produced style sheets for her UX designer to implement onto the app.
