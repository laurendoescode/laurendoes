Icon Design Client: Honor, Personal

Honor - open compeition

Icon and wallpaper designs for Honor 6’s launch into Europe

The brief

To launch Honor’s new phone in Europe, Honor held a competition to design their new phone's icon interface and wallpapers. They wanted the design to be based around the theme #forthebrave.

The process

I interpreted brave as conquering new frontiers, both in new digital technology and geographical frontiers into Europe. Graphics are clean with a bold palette to inject vibrancy into the interface. For the set of wallpapers I concentrated on anabstract map of each European city. Illustrating London’s transport system for the submission.
