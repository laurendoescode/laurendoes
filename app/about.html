about

Hi, hello, Wie geht’s?

I’m a self-taught Graphic Designer with a keen interest in Psychology. Over the last few years I've enjoyed working on a wide range of project from white papers for Microsoft to illustrating a new psychology test for MIT and Columbia University.

Originally from the UK, I am currently living in Berlin. I've worked both nationally and internationally with clients across the UK, Europe, America and China.

If you have any layout or illustration needs send me a quick email at hello@laurendo.es
